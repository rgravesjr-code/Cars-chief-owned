1959 AH 100-6 Silver
Excellent example
Year: 1959  ·  Color: Silver  ·  Acquired: 1966  ·  Condition: Excellent

The silver 100-6 was the best of Roger's three Austin Healeys of this generation — rated excellent, it represented the Big Healey at its finest. By 1966, Roger had owned or driven multiple Healeys and knew exactly what to look for. This silver example delivered everything the marque promised: a sonorous inline six, beautiful proportions, and the kind of open-air touring experience that only a proper British roadster could provide.

The Austin Healey 100-6 in silver would have been a striking sight on 1960s American roads — rare, elegant, and distinctly un-American in the best possible way. Roger's three-Healey run through the mid-sixties shows a real passion for the marque, and this excellent silver car was the crown jewel of that collection.
