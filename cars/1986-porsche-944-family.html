Porsche 944

Champagne · Excellent · "Sorry I sold it" · 1992

Year: 1986  ·  Color: Champagne  ·  Acquired: 1992  ·  Condition: Excellent

Four words in Roger's notes say everything: "Sorry I sold it." Of the hundreds of cars that passed through his hands over six decades, this champagne 1986 Porsche 944 is one of the very few that prompted genuine regret at its departure. That note of wistfulness speaks volumes — this was a car that got under his skin.

The 944 in champagne would have been a subtle, sophisticated color choice — not showy, but undeniably classy. The naturally-aspirated 944 was already a wonderful driver's car: balanced, precise, beautifully built, with the transaxle layout giving it near-perfect 50/50 weight distribution. Roger also owned the more powerful 944 Turbo version in the sports car section — but this champagne example, rated excellent, was clearly special in its own right.

The cars we regret selling stay with us. This champagne Porsche 944 is Roger's "one that got away."
