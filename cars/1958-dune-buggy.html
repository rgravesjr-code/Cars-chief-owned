Sports Cars · 1958

VW Dune Buggy

Red — traded for the 1961 Corvette in 1968 · Hell, MI

Year: 1958  ·  Color: Red  ·  Acquired: 1968  ·  Fate: Traded for 1961 Corvette

Not every car in Roger's collection was about refinement — the red VW Dune Buggy was pure fun. Based on a Volkswagen Beetle floorpan, the Meyers Manx-style dune buggy craze swept America in the late 1960s, turning humble Beetles into lightweight, open-air off-road machines. Roger's red example was exactly the kind of machine you'd take to the hills around Hell, Michigan — and he did exactly that, with Roger Jr. and Randy aboard for the adventure.

The photo in Roger's collection shows the buggy mid-action at Hell, MI, capturing the kind of carefree fun that defined the era. It wasn't a car you kept forever — it was a car you enjoyed thoroughly and then traded for something entirely different. In this case, that something was a 1961 Corvette, which says everything about Roger's automotive priorities.
