Harley-Davidson Ultra Classic

Loaded with GPS · "Great touring bike" · eBay 2007

Year: 2005  ·  Type: Touring  ·  Source: eBay 2007  ·  Equipment: GPS, Full Touring Package

The Harley-Davidson Electra Glide Ultra Classic is the flagship of the Harley touring lineup — a full-dress touring motorcycle with a fairing, saddlebags, top box, and every comfort accessory available. Roger's 2005 example came loaded, including GPS navigation, making it the most technologically equipped motorcycle in his collection. His note simply: "Great touring bike."

The Ultra Classic represents the long-distance cruising tradition that Harley-Davidson had built since the 1960s. With a Twin Cam 88 V-twin, a comfortable riding position, and enough luggage capacity for a week's worth of gear, the Ultra Classic was built for crossing America in style. For someone who had ridden to the Smokies on a Moto Guzzi and to Niagara Falls on a Honda Super Hawk, the Ultra Classic was the natural endpoint of a lifetime of touring rides.
