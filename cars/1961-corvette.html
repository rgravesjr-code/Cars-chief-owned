Sports Cars · 1961

Corvette

Red · Tri-power · Acquired 1968 · Mediocre

Year: 1961  ·  Color: Red  ·  Acquired: 1968  ·  Engine: Tri-power  ·  Condition: Mediocre

The 1961 Corvette was the final year of the first-generation C1 body, and Roger's red example came equipped with the desirable "tri-power" setup — three two-barrel carburetors feeding the 283 or 327 V8, a factory performance option that gave the car genuine muscle car credentials alongside its sports car character. Roger traded the dune buggy to get it in 1968.

Despite the exciting specification, Roger rated this car as mediocre — a reminder that condition matters as much as specification. Even a tri-power Corvette can disappoint if it's not properly sorted. Still, it filled the garage until something better came along, and it represented Roger's continued love affair with America's sports car that had started with the dark green '65.
