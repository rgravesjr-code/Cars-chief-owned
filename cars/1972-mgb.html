MGB Roadster

Orange — sold to Roger Jr. in 1985

Year: 1972  ·  Color: Orange  ·  Acquired: 1985  ·  Fate: Sold to Roger Jr.

The orange 1972 MGB roadster has a special place in this collection — it was sold to Roger's son, Roger Jr., making it a family car in every sense. The MGB was already a classic by the time Roger acquired this one in 1985: a long-running British roadster that had been in production since 1962, the MGB offered honest, affordable sports car motoring with genuine character.

The 1972 model year MGB was one of the transitional cars — still with the chrome bumpers that collectors prize, before the federally-mandated rubber bumpers arrived in 1974. In orange, it was a vivid and eye-catching machine. That it ended up with Roger Jr. shows how car enthusiasm tends to run in families.
