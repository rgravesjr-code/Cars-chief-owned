Kawasaki Drifter 1500

Indian look-alike · Brand new · 1999

Year: 1999  ·  Engine: 1500cc V-twin  ·  Purchased: Brand new · 1999

The Kawasaki Drifter 1500 was Kawasaki's bold attempt to capture the spirit of the classic Indian motorcycle in a modern, reliable package. With its deeply valanced fenders, springer-style front fork, and retro styling that echoed the great Indians of the 1940s and 1950s, the Drifter 1500 was one of the most visually distinctive cruisers of its era. Roger bought one brand new in 1999, demonstrating his eye for the unusual and striking.

The Drifter's 1500cc V-twin engine delivered strong, lazy power well-suited to cruising, while Kawasaki's engineering gave it the reliability that the original Indian — long since out of business — could never quite manage consistently. It was a beautiful machine that turned heads everywhere it went, combining nostalgic American styling with modern Japanese build quality. An unusual choice in a collection dominated by Harleys — and all the more interesting for it.
