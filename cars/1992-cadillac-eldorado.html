Eldorado
America's luxury personal coupe
Year: 1992  ·  Color: Pearl Crème  ·  Acquired: 2000  ·  Condition: Sharp, Loaded

The Cadillac Eldorado has always been America's personal luxury coupe par excellence, and the 1992 model was the penultimate year of the 1986-generation body — a clean, long-hood, short-deck design powered by Cadillac's Northstar V8 system in later variants or the 4.9-litre V8 in earlier cars. In pearl crème, Roger's Eldorado was a striking and elegant machine — that colour suits the long, low coupe profile perfectly.

Roger described it as "sharp and loaded," which captures the Eldorado experience well. These were luxury cars in the truest sense: every available comfort and technology option, wrapped in a design that spoke of genuine prestige. The Eldorado was a car for someone who had arrived — and by 2000, Roger's collection showed exactly that kind of confident, quality-focused taste.
