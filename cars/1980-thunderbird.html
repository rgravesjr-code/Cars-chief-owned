1980 Thunderbird

Black · Brand new · 1980

Year: 1980  ·  Color: Black  ·  Purchased: Brand new · 1980

The 1980 Thunderbird was the second generation of the downsized "Fox Body" T-Bird that Ford had introduced in 1977, and Roger bought one brand new in black. The downsizing was a response to the fuel crisis of the 1970s — the massive land yachts of the earlier T-Bird generations gave way to a trimmer, more European-influenced design that shared its platform with the Ford Fairmont and Mercury Zephyr.

In black, the 1980 Thunderbird was a sleek, coupe-styled personal luxury car with the kind of formal roofline and landau top options that were fashionable in the era. Buying it new showed Roger's appreciation for a fresh car — the smell of new leather, the warranty, the knowledge that you knew the car's full history from day one. The T-Bird nameplate carried genuine prestige, and this was a comfortable, well-equipped American coupe for its time.
