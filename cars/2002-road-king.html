Year: 2002  ·  Color: White  ·  Acquired: 2001  ·  Type: Touring

The Harley-Davidson Road King Classic is the quintessential American touring motorcycle — a full-dress FLHRC with leather saddlebags, a large front fairing, and all the visual presence that only a Harley can deliver. Roger's white example, acquired in 2001, was part of a remarkable period when he owned multiple Harleys simultaneously, rotating through Heritage Springers, Road Kings, and eventually the Ultra Classic.

The Road King Classic's defining feature is its combination of classic styling with genuine touring ability. The removable windshield, genuine leather saddlebags, and the distinctive sound of the Twin Cam 88 V-twin made it one of Harley's most beloved touring models. In white, it had a clean, pure look that suited the classic Road King proportions beautifully. Roger called it beautiful — and it was.
