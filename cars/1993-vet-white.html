Corvette Coupe

White · 30,000 miles · 6-speed · "One of the nicest I've owned" — eBay 2006

Year: 1993  ·  Color: White  ·  Miles: 30,000  ·  Trans: 6-Speed Manual  ·  Source: eBay

Roger called this white 1993 Corvette "one of the nicest I've owned" — high praise from a man who owned well over a dozen Corvettes across six decades. Found on eBay in 2006 with just 30,000 miles and equipped with the desirable 6-speed manual gearbox, this C4 Corvette represented the platform at its absolute best: sorted, low-mileage, and well preserved.

The 1993 Corvette came with the LT1 V8 engine, a significant update from the earlier L98 that brought 300 horsepower, reverse-flow cooling, and a far more modern engine management system. Paired with the ZF 6-speed manual — one of the finest gearboxes Corvette ever offered — it was a magnificent driver's car. Low miles on a C4 are relatively rare; finding one this clean on eBay was the kind of deal Roger had a real talent for spotting.

This car joins a distinguished list of Roger's eBay Corvette finds, a testament to his skill at finding genuine low-mileage examples at fair prices. That he regarded it as one of the nicest in a collection of this depth says everything about its quality.
